Service Record Management System

Project Info

A multilingual software for storing information of all levels of employees of the Forest Department, under the Forest Ministry of Bangladesh.

  1. Multiple levels of users to enter, review, and approve data.
  2. 20-25 categories of information stored per employee.
  3. Generates comprehensive reports in various formats.
Panels:
  1. Admin Panel
  2. Entry Panel
  3. Reviewer Panel
  4. DFO Panel
Key Features:
  1. Complete employee profile entry
  2. Review by Reviewer Panel
  3. Final approval by DFO Panel
  4. Generation of reports in multiple formats
  5. Custom configurations

Client

Forest Department, Bangladesh

Skill Used

PHP, Laravel, Livewire, MySQL

URL
