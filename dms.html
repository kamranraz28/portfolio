Distribution Management System

Project Info

A complete ERP system software with a mobile application for Salextra Limited, the mother distributor company of Nokia Mobile in Bangladesh.

The warehouse uploads the stocks regularly. Distributors can create orders for themselves, and TSO/TSM can make orders for the distributors they are aligned with. The accounts panel checks and reviews the order and approves it if everything is fine. Then, the warehouse uploads the respective products for that order and delivers the order with the delivery information. After that, the distributor can see their new products and receive them as a primary purchase. Distributors sell the products to retailers as secondary sales, and then retailers sell products to customers as tertiary sales. All these processes are monitored through respective cross-checks.

When a retailer sells a mobile phone to a customer, they activate an E-warranty for that mobile. During the warranty period, if the mobile faces any issues, the distributor and retailer can claim a replacement and return it. The application and completion of these processes are handled by this software.
