Field Force Tracking System

Project Info

A comprehensive solution designed to manage and track field visits by field forces. It integrates a real-time GPS tracking to ensure smooth operations. The system allows authorities to schedule visits, real-time track force locations, and verify attendance with selfies while providing a notification system for missed visits or updates.

Key Features:

  1. Real-time GPS Tracking: Tracks Force's movement from 9 AM to 6 PM every 10 minutes, providing live path data
  2. Selfie Attendance Verification: Force take a selfie upon arriving at the field location, which is time-stamped and tagged with GPS coordinates.
  3. Authorities Notifications: Authorities receive alerts if an force misses a scheduled field visit
  4. Google Maps Path Tracking: Authorities can track force movements on a map, seeing the exact route taken throughout the day.

Panels:

  1. Admin Panel
  2. Field Force Panel

Skill Used

PHP, Laravel, Restful API, MySQL
