Covid-19 Vaccination System

Project Info

A software to register for the Covid Vaccine online with NID, Informing about schedule date and time after registration via E-mail. And a reminder E-mail will send automatically to the user just before the day at 9PM of vaccination date. After the vaccination date pass over the vaccination status of this user will update to "vaccinated".

Panels:
  1. Admin Panel
  2. User Panel
  3. Vaccine Center Panel
Key Features:

Admin Panel:
  1. Add different levels of user
  2. Set the vaccination center and amount of vaccine per day per center
  3. Active/Inactive Center
  4. Track Vaccination Process over the country
Vaccination Center Panel:
  1. Apply for Vaccine
  2. Active/Inactive for new schedule
  3. Patient history
Patient Panel:
  1. Search their vaccination progress by NID
  2. Online registration and automatic vaccine card.
  3. Getting vaccination Notifications via E-mail or Phone
